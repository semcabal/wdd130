Moyobamba Peru

Moyobamba is the capital of the Peruvian department of San Martín, located in the high jungle, known as the "City of Orchids." Founded in 1540, it is the first Spanish city in the Amazon and an important cultural and tourist center. It is noted for its rich biodiversity, especially its orchids, its historic tradition of exploration, and its natural beauty, which includes the Mayo River and nearby waterfalls.
